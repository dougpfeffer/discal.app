DISCAL: A Discord Events calendar generator.

If you are a member of a Discord with this app installed, you have two options for subscribing to the event feed:

1. Subscribe to the full list of events:

https://discal.app/guilds/<GUILD ID>/events.ics

2. Subscibe to the list of events you're "interested" in:

https://discal.app/guilds/<GUILD ID>/users/<YOUR DISCORD ID>/events.ics

Be sure to subscribe to these URLs in your Calendar app.

It should have a New Calendar Subscription option of some kind. Don't just download the file and double click it, that won't subscribe you to future updates.
